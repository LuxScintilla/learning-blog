Here, I have dedicated a space to keep a weekly diary of my learning progress with the ODIN Project.

I have done my best to keep track of the extra resources I used along the way, which are listed along with the subjects learned on a given week. There will be ofcourse the odd video I watch in the bus or before bed that might be missing from the list.

The only experience I had, prior to my start with the ODIN Project, was some basic HTML and CSS. On average I manage to study about 4 to 5 hours a day, 5 days a week, and sometimes a bit extra on a weekend if I can. As I am a single mum, I fit all my learning around taking care of my little boy, who at the time of me starting this journey is 4 years old.
